Founder & Consultant

SUNSET is an educational consulting and training company located in Stockholm, Sweden. We offer a wide range of services related to education abroad, with a special focus on higher education in the Nordic countries.

SUNSET is committed to building a bridge between the Nordic countries and the rest of the world. Our aim is to introduce universities in Nordic countries to the students who are seeking high quality education, and to help and guide more students to achieve their goals.

At the same time, SUNSET also wants to build an international student community in order to provide a platform for students to help and exchange ideas with each other.
